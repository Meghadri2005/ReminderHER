What is PCOD?

PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease) is a condition where ovaries produce many immature or partially mature eggs, leading to hormonal imbalance.

Common Symptoms:

  • Irregular periods or no periods at all
  • Acne, hair loss, or unwanted hair growth
  • Weight gain and mood swings

Types of PCOD:

  • Insulin-resistant PCOD
  • Inflammatory PCOD
  • Pill-induced PCOD

Recovery Tips:

  • Exercise regularly (like walking or yoga)
  • Eat a balanced diet (less sugar and oily food)
  • Stay positive and reduce stress
